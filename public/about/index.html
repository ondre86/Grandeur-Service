About Grandeur Service

Grandeur Service is a luxury transportation company serving the Memphis metropolitan area.
We're born and raised in Memphis, with decades of professional driving & dispatching experience across the country.

You can trust us to navigate you around Downtown Memphis, Midtown, South Memphis, East Memphis, Shelby County, and the surrounding areas with ease and efficiency.

Our range extends in a 300 mile radius, encompassing cities such as:

  • Nashville, Tennesee
  • St. Louis, Missouri
  • Springfield, Missouri
  • Birmingham, Alabama
  • Tuscaloosa, Alabama
  • Jackson, Mississippi
  • Little Rock, Arkansas


We believe in providing our clients the highest standard of luxury and professionalism, while developing and maintaining close relationships along the way.

We aren't just another black car service catering to each and every person who calls.

We're your drivers, here for you at every turn.
