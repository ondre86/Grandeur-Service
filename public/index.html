Frequently Asked Questions

How does the booking process work for black car service?

New clients can use our online booking form to request a reservation. Once submitted, our Memphis-based dispatch team will confirm your black car or limo service details and provide next steps.

How can I trust my chauffeur?

All Grandeur Service drivers pass comprehensive background checks and are hand-selected for professionalism, driving record, and client satisfaction. You may request driver info after securing your reservation.

What happens if my driver is unavailable?

All clients have a preference list of drivers.

If your driver is unavailable at the time of your reservation, the task will fall to the next driver you are most comfortable with.

What rules apply during rides?

The following rules wil be enforced on every ride:

  • No Smoking
  • No Eating

Should you break any of these rules during your ride, additional fees may apply.

Can I book a black car for out-of-town travel?

Yes. We provide long-distance and intercity black car service from Memphis to surrounding cities and beyond. Perfect for business trips, family travel, or weekend getaways.

Are there any extra fees for long-distance or cleanup?

Extra fees may apply for:

  • One-way rides exceeding 300 miles (30¢ per extra mile)
  • Food or drink spills (cleaning fee varies)
  • Bodily fluid accidents (cleaning fee based on severity)
How often are vehicles cleaned?

Our vehicles are fully detailed as needed.

We take cleanliness very seriously in order to provide experiences of the highest quality.

Why is there a two-hour minimum for reservations?

We don't want to be another Uber or taxi service. We want to build lasting, trusting relationships with our clients.

Our two-hour minimum helps us deliver top-tier private chauffeur service in Memphis while covering operational costs and offering a premium experience.
